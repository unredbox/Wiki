Electronic Parts

This sections is for electronics and parts that need replacement

Tripplite UPS Battery

Battery is installed with B.B. Battery HR1234W VRLA Rechargeable Battery (12V,34WPC/15min./1.30VPC/25°C). These batteries are known to pillow and cause fires. Replacing with a better alternative is recommended.

Recommended replacement would be a good quality 12V9Ah SLA or 12V7Ah SLA Battery. These parts can be found on Amazon or eBay.

How to replace battery safely

MAKE SURE THE UPS IS UNPLUGGED BEFORE REPLACING THE BATTERY TO AVOID ELECTRIC SHOCK

  1. There should be a Philips screw at the bottom of the UPS that should be undone to be able to slide the cover out
  2. Unplug the negative connector first, then positive connector.
  3. Take the replacement battery and plug the positive terminal to the red connector, then negative terminal to the black connector. Take caution as there is risk of electric shock since most capacitors have been down for a while
  4. Test the UPS by turning it on. If the screen shows, then battery replacement is done and the cover can go back on. If it still doesn't turn on, repair or replacement of the Tripplite may be required.

IceQube IQ2200VS

This section contains parts for replacing the Blower Motor Assembly. You will need to order these parts directly from IceQube Sales at 1-888-867-8234. Serial Number is located on the side, as you will need to provide this. Refer to the sticker located on the AC unit.

Single Blower Motor

Note that this part if was manufactured before 2012 was recalled for causing fires. Replacement of this part is required.

Complete single-wheel blower assembly used as the evaporator blower. It continuously operates to circulate cold air into the enclosure.

  • Part Number: BLWR1011-A
  • Location: Top Right
  • How to Remove (Long Philips Screw Driver Required): 
    1. 3 Left Philips Screws
    2. Right Philips Screws
    3. Cut the zip tie holding it on and disconnect the connector 

Double Blower Motor

Complete two-wheel blower assembly used as the condenser blower. It moves hot air out of the air conditioner. This piece is attached to the back of the casing, which must be removed and disconnected from the main unit before setting to the side.

  • Part Number: BLWR1007-A
  • Location: Bottom Left

Carousel Parts

This section should be for parts related to the carousel

Carousel Belts

Per measurements, the belt is 57.09" in length and 0.98" in width with 290 teeth. 

Replacement Options:

(Confirmed) Gates PowerGrip GT 5MR-1450-25:
Note: Part is no longer in production. Recommends PowerGrip GT2 5MR-1450-25 as a replacement from the manufacturer. You can find these parts new on eBay if you want the exact part, but caution to be alert and verify the part is new and not used as used parts are unverified if they are any good.

(Alternative) Biedler's Belts:
Note: Original part uses PowerGrip GT belts while the new part uses PowerGrip GT2, which uses a different material.

Differences Observed:

  • Heat Resistant
  • Fiberglass Tensile Cord
  • Chloroprene Core Material

It is possible to use another belt as long as it has the same measurements:

1450 - Length in Millimeters
25 - Width in Millimeters
5M - Belt Pitch/Tooth Profile
Usually sold as a timing belt

V-Belt Guys sells a belt which has been successfully tested and works to replace the carousel belt at a lower cost than OEM replacement. The model received has been reported as a D&D Synchro Power Belt HTD 1450-5m.

Disc Cases

At least five versions of cases have been observed with differences apparently only being cosmetic. 

One version, shown the lower left, includes a barcode on the reverse side: “RDBX00728.”

(The addition of the UPC code might have allowed a retailer to more easily sell a missing replacement case.)

There were employee cases that were marked Blue, Orange, and Red

 

Employee Cases

Employee cases were to indicate whether they were redeploys, redistributes, and thins. These may still be inside kiosks and is rare to find one of these.

Grey

Any discs after Grey were redeploys, which were removed and randomly added in other kiosks in the area. These were phased out in 2020 in favor of thinning redeploys instead

Blue

Any discs after Blue were redistributes, which another kiosk would ask for specific movies and were tracked as well which discs go to which kiosk asking for the disc.

Orange

Any discs after Orange were thins, which were shipped back to the company to either be destroyed, donated, or sold to retailers. Dollar Generals have been known to sell thinned out discs in good condition.
